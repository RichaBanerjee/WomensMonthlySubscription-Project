TUTORIALS

Watch these easy tutorials and learn how to use sanitary napkins, tampons and cups within minutes!.

How to use tampons

1. Follow all labeled directions.

2. Wash your hands before and after using a tampon.

3. Only use tampons when you have your period and Change each tampon every 4 to 8 hours.

How to use pads

1. Stick the pad in your underwear using the sticky strip on the back. Some reusable pads are held in place with snaps or the elastic in your underwear.

2. Change your pad every few hours, or when it's soaked with blood.

3. Wrap used pads in the wrapper or toilet paper and throw them in the trash.

How to use menstrual cups

1. Check that the air holes at the top of your cup are open. Wah your hands.

2. Get comfy. You can insert the cup while sitting, standing or squatting. Spreading your legs will help with a successfull and comfortable insertion. Relax.

3. Keep it rolled up and guide it rim first into the vagina.

Some tips to maintain menstrual hygiene

1. Keep your genital area clean.

2. Change your menstrual products regularly.

3. Track and monitor your period. Also, visit your gynaecologist for annual checkup.
